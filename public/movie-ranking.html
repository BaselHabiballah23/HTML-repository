The best Movies Ever watched

My top 3 movies I have ever seen:

  1. Peaky Blinders

    "Peaky Blinders" is a British television crime drama series set in post-World War I Birmingham, England. It follows the exploits of the Shelby crime family, led by the ambitious and cunning Thomas Shelby, as they navigate the treacherous world of gang warfare and politics to establish their dominance in the city. The series is known for its stylish cinematography, compelling characters, and gripping storytelling

  2. The Circle

    "The Circle" is a science fiction thriller film that explores the dark side of technology and social media. In the movie, a young woman named Mae Holland lands a dream job at a powerful tech company called The Circle, but she soon discovers the company's intrusive surveillance practices and the potential dangers of an all-encompassing digital world. The film delves into themes of privacy, ethics, and the consequences of an interconnected society.

  3. The Pianist

    "The Pianist" is a biographical war drama film directed by Roman Polanski. It tells the true story of Władysław Szpilman, a Jewish pianist in Warsaw during World War II. The movie depicts his struggle for survival amidst the Nazi occupation and the Holocaust, showcasing the resilience of the human spirit in the face of unimaginable adversity. "The Pianist" is a poignant and powerful portrayal of one man's harrowing journey through one of the darkest chapters in history.
